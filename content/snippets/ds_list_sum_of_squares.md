ds_list_sum_of_squares
======================

The sum of squares, or sum of squared deviation scores,
is a measure of variability in a data set. 

$$\large TSS=\Sigma(x_i-\bar{x})^2
\qquad \bar{x}=\frac{\Sigma{x}}{n}$$
